Alimayu Park

Hi, my name is Alimayu Park, and I am an NY-based actor who is also passionate about filmmaking. I've gone to LaGuardia High School for 3 years as a drama major and have received a fruitful education in on-stage acting and ensemble work. I've also taken a Film Literature class at LaGuardia for 1 year, which sparked a new passion for filmmaking and led me on a journey to become a part of the film industry and begin creating my own films.
